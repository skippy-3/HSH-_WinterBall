Welcome

An Evening To Remember

Once a year, our hall trades chalk dust for candlelight. Winter Ball is Hopefield High's celebration of everything our school community has built together this year — a night of good music, good company, and a little bit of glamour before the winter chill sets back in. Come dressed in your finest semi-formal attire, bring your dancing shoes, and get ready for rhythm, radiance, and a room full of familiar faces.
